Title: To The Light

Date: May 29, 2019

Medium: Video Game

Collaborators: Telea Rendon

Link: Link to Itch.io

Description

To The Light is a 2D game based on the experience of waking up, as a child, from a nightmare and running through the dark to your parents room. I choose this experience because itâ€™s one of the few childhood memories that really stand out for me. The most distinctive parts of my memory was turning on the lights as I went to my parents room. I turned that into the core mechanics of To The Light. The player must travel through the game by turning on the room and hallway lights in order to avoid the monster. The monster can threaten the player by turning off any light that the player is not in. Areas of safety change dramatically and the player must use timing and long term planning to complete each level.
