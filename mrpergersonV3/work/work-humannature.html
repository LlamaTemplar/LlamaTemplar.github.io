Human Nature

Previous Next

Date: April 4th, 2020

Medium: 3D Animation

Duration: 30 seconds

Technology: Maya

Link: to YouTube

Description

Human nature is an animation that shows an exaggerated view of the growth of our species and how we spread throughout our environment. The animation starts with a green landscape but the composition becomes slowly engulfed by the rising skyscrapers. By the end of the video, there is a new environment, one that is rigid and dark. It’s easy to create structures that aren’t compatible with nature, but this is not healthy for us. I believe that nature is important for our mental health and our cities should seek to be harmonious with nature, rather than overrule it.
