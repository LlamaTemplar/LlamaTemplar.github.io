Description

A bite-sized horror experience, Tezca: In The Shadows is a student project produced in the Animation & Illustration program at SJSU. You play as Petra, a mexican soldier who falls into a hidden atez temple. Petra must unlock the puzzle to escape the darkness while avoiding a mysterious but deadly spirit

My role

As a gameplay programmer, I worked along side with two other programmers to develop Tezca: In the shadows in Unity3D. My primary responsibility was creating the cutscene system, as well as, the hiding spot mechanic. Early in the development, I worked on the player logic and movement code. This involved working with the modelers and animations to troubleshoot Unity animation importing. I gained valuable experience in the maya-to-unity pipline and with Unity's animation system.
