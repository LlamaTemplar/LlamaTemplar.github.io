Cole Pergerson

When I was five years old, I finished my first Lego set. Little did I know, at the time, that was my first step into creating imaginative worlds and immersive scenarios. Today, I'm creating interactive computer narratives while studying Digital Media Art at SJSU. I've published my games on Itch.io and you can see my other creative work on my website. I'm very active in the game development on campus; I have held multiple workshops and other events to help others develop their own successful games. I'm currently looking for opportunities to show off my work or get guidance from a mentor or job.
