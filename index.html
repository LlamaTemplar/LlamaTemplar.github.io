About Me

(っ◔◡◔)っ Welcome

My name is Cole Pergerson and I create video games. My primary work exists within the realm of digital art, specifically computer interactivity. I focus in game design and development, however, I'm not shy to explore other media within digital art. In addition, I'm a web developer with experience in full-stack development and web graphics using ThreeJS. I’m a confident visionary in that I seek to find new and creative ways to solve problems. Furthermore, I’m open-minded to change and I listen to what others have to say. I can work well with others because I seek to understand what others want and I confront any tension. When in leadership positions, I focus on the welfare of my teammates to ensure that there are no team conflicts and everyone is getting part of something they want.
